Plant Care

Green thumbs made simple

Caring for plants shouldn't be stressful. We provide simple, actionable instructions for water, light, temperature, and feeding for every single plant in our collection.

1

Check Soil Moisture

Insert your finger 2 inches into the soil. If dry, it's time to water.

2

Position for Indirect Light

Most plants love bright, indirect sun. Protect them from direct afternoon rays.

3

Mist Regularly

Tropical indoor varieties adore high humidity. Give their leaves a gentle spray twice a week.
